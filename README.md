###  Best Time to Sell
- **Insight:** Customer demand peaks during the **evening**.  
- **Why it matters:** More customers at this time means staff might need extra help and drinks should be prepared faster.  
- **Recommendation:** Schedule more staff and prep popular drinks in advance for busy evenings.
